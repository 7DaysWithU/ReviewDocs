# 应用文写作(小作文)

## 1 总览

### 1.1 步骤

* 审题三事项

  a. 背景  
  b. 体裁 + 内容要求（描述）  
  c. 语域（正式/非正式）+ 格式

* 三段式简易思路

  **首段 1-2 句：**  
  a. 首给熟人：首句寒暄，二句目的；  
  b. 写给陌生人或机构：自我介绍和目的。
  
  **中间段 4-6 句：**  
  按题目要求写相应的内容，并辅以感谢、强调、对比、建议、说明等功能句完成写作。
  
  **尾段 1-2 句：**  
  （再次）致歉、感谢、期待、展望等。

### 1.2 格式

* 书信

  ```Text
  +------------------------------------------+
  | Dear xxx,                                |
  |     [开头]I am writing to ......          |
  |     [正文]............................... |
  | ........................................ |
  | ............................             |
  |     [结尾]............................... |
  | ..................                       |
  |                         Yours sincerely, |
  |                                  Li Ming |
  +------------------------------------------+
  ```

* 通知

  ```Text
  +------------------------------------------+
  |                 Notice                   |
  |                      Monday, December 20 |
  |     [开头]This notice is for .......      |
  |     [正文]............................... |
  | ........................................ |
  | ............................             |
  |     [结尾]............................... |
  | ..................                       |
  |                              发通知的单位 |
  +------------------------------------------+
  ```

## 2 模板

### 2.1 道歉信

* 开头

  很高兴回想起在之前...
  > $\text{I'm quite pleased to recall the past 时间 when ...}$
  
  我写信的目的是...
  > $\text{I'm writing for the purpose of doing ...}$

* 正文

  **按题目要求写相应的内容**

  我是如此粗心，以至于我才发现/忘了...
  > $\text{I'm so careless and I did not found ...}$

* 结尾

  最后，我真诚地希望您能站在我的角度考虑一下并接受我的道歉
  > $\text{Finally, I truly hope that you can stand in my shoes and accept my apologies.}$

  再次为引发的不便向您道歉
  > $\text{Once again, I am sorry for any inconveniences caused.}$

### 2.2 通知

* 开头

  很高兴...

  这是个用于...的通知
  > <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-variant-numeric: lining-nums;"> This notice is for the purpose of doing ... </span>

* 正文

  某事将计划从12月20号14点在某地开始，并持续(大约)7天
  > <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-variant-numeric: lining-nums;"> 某事 is scheduled to start at 14 o'clock on December 20 in 某地 and last for (approximately) 7 days </span>

* 结尾

  12月21日在某地处对申请者进行面试
  > $\text{There will be an interview for applicants on December 21 in 某地 }$

### 2.3 建议

* 结尾

  如果有其他建议或要求的话，请直接告诉我
  > $\text{If you have any better suggestions or requirements, please fell free to inform me.}$

## 3 功能句

* 强调

  > $\text{It is 狗 that 狗}$

  ***示例：***

  > $\text{It is practice that make perfect.}$

* 应该做某事

  > $\text{It is advisable for sb. to do sth.}$ </br>
  > $\text{We are supposed to do sth.}$ </br>
  > $\text{It is exceedingly necessary for sb. to do sth.}$ </br>
  > 某人应该做某事

* 使得我们

  > $\text{enable us to do sth.}$

* 自我介绍

  > $\text{As + 身份 + 定语从句}$ </br>
  > 作为一个(身份)，我....

  这样写的目的是使写信人与收信人双方产生联系

* 写信为了

  > $\text{I am writing [for the purpose of] doing sth. [since/because] + 从句}$ </br>
  > 我写信是为了sth.，因为...

* 我认为

  > $\text{I am [convinced/deem/reckon/believed/contended] that}$

* 狗的特点是

  > $\text{狗 is characterized by 特点1 and 特点2}$  
  > $\text{狗 is characterized by the fact that 从句}$
  > * 经典示例：中国的显著特征在于其经济的蓬勃和繁荣  
  > <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-variant-numeric: lining-nums;"> China is characterized by the economic boom and prosperity. </span>

* 希望你享受旅行

  > $\text{I hope that you can enjoy a comfortable and fruitful trip.}$

* 期待您的早日回信

  > $\text{I'm looking forward to your favorable reply at your earliest convenience}$

* 国际生 $\text{International students}$

  > <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-variant-numeric: lining-nums;"> Which can bring you the opportunities to make Chinese friends, inspire your passion for campus life and enable you adapt to life in China better. </span>  
  > 这可以给你带来结交中国朋友的机会，激发你对校园生活的热情，让你更好地适应中国的生活。

* 作为一个大四准备考研的学生，我非常忙碌，因为我把几乎所有时间都投入到考试的准备上了

  > <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 1.2em; font-variant-numeric: lining-nums;"> As a senior student who is preparing for the national entrance examination for postgraduate studies, I am exceedingly busy because I devote nearly all my time to preparing for the test. </span>
