# 石雷鹏小作文预测

## 冲刺背诵1：通知/告示（介绍+邀请）

- **题目要求**：以学生会名义，为国际新生写一则欢迎派对的通告。
- **相关主题词汇**：`welcome party` (欢迎会)， `international students` (国际学生)， `campus life` (校园生活)， `cultural introduction` (文化介绍)。
- **信件目的功能句框架**
  - **目的**：`This notice / announcement is for the purpose of inviting you to [活动名称].`
    > `This notice / announcement is for the purpose of inviting you to attend a welcome party.`
- **内容阐述功能句框架**
  - **活动时间地点**：`The [活动名称] is scheduled to start at [时间] on [日期] in [地点] and last for approximately [时长].`
    > `the party is scheduled to start at 19:00 on June 20 in the Center of Students' Activities and last for approximately two hours.`
  - **活动特色**：`In addition, this [活动名称] is characterized by [特色1] and [特色2], including [具体活动].`
    > `In addition, this party is characterized by a variety of interesting activities, including singing, dancing, Chinese culture introduction, and fun competitions.`
- **结尾呼吁功能句框架**
  - **呼吁**：`To conclude, it is advisable for you to participate in this [活动名称], which will bring you opportunities to [好处1], arouse your passion for [相关领域], and help you [好处2].`
    > `it is advisable for you to participate in this party, which will bring you opportunities to make friends, arouse your passion for the campus life, and help you know China better.`
    > `Finally, we are looking forward to your participation.`

## 冲刺背诵2：推荐信（建议+说明）

- **题目要求**：为寻找AI工具提高学习效率的外国朋友写推荐邮件。
- **相关主题词汇**：`AI tools` (AI工具)， `learning efficiency` (学习效率)， `information collection` (信息搜集)。
- **信件目的功能句框架**
  - **目的**：`I am quite pleased to hear that [对方背景]. Today, I am writing to make a recommendation.`
    > `I am quite pleased to hear that you are looking for AI tools to promote learning efficiency. Today, I am writing to make a recommendation.`
- **内容阐述功能句框架**
  - **核心建议**：`It is advisable for you to [建议行动] since it is by [行动] that you can [达到的效果].`
    > `It is advisable for you to try out several AI tools in learning since it is by trying them out that you can know which one can best suit your needs.`
  - **个人案例引入**：`A case in point is myself. As a [你的身份] who [正在做的事], I [你的做法].`
    > `A case in point is myself. As a senior who is preparing for the national entrance exams for postgraduate studies, I tried ERNIE Bot, Tongyi, and Kimi.`
  - **推荐对象特点**：`In the end, I chose [推荐对象] which is characterized by its [突出特点].`
    > `In the end, I chose Kimi which is characterized by its excellent performance in information collection.`
  - **推荐对象好处**：`This [推荐对象] can bring me [好处1], inspire my passion for [相关领域], and help me [好处2].`
    > `This AI tool can bring me high efficiency, inspire my passion for learning, and help me save time and energy.`
- **结尾表达功能句框架**
  - **结尾**：`I am looking forward to discussing more details of [主题] after you use them.`

## 冲刺背诵3：回复邀请邮件（介绍+感谢）

- **题目要求**：回复教授关于机器人比赛的邀请邮件，介绍机器人功能。
- **相关主题词汇**：`robot` (机器人)， `companionship` (陪伴)， `elderly citizens` (老年人)， `emergency calls` (紧急呼叫)。
- **信件目的功能句框架**
  - **目的**：`I’m so glad to receive your invitation and we’re perfectly willing to participate. Today, I am writing for the purpose of [信件目的].`
    > `I’m so glad to receive your invitation and we’re perfectly willing to participate. Today, I am writing for the purpose of introducing the main functions of our robot.`
- **内容阐述功能句框架**
  - **项目背景**：`Compared to the past years, [某个社会现象或趋势]. Thus, we have [采取的行动].`
    > `Compared to the past years, both the proportion and number of elderly citizens in modern society have increased significantly. Thus, we have designed a robot to provide companionship for them at home.`
  - **介绍特点**：`The [介绍对象] is characterized by [特点1] and [特点2], such as [具体功能1], [具体功能2], and [具体功能3].`
    > `The robot is characterized by diverse and powerful functions, such as cleaning rooms, singing, telling stories, chatting, and making emergency calls.`
  - **阐述意义**：`We deem that it can bring the [目标人群] [好处1], inspire their passion for [相关领域], and help them [好处2].`
    > `We deem that it can bring the elderly joy, inspire their passion for life, and help them ease the loneliness.`
- **结尾请求功能句框架**
  - **请求**：`We would be grateful if you could inform us of [需要对方提供的信息].`

## 冲刺背诵4：感谢+邀请信

- **题目要求**：感谢在国外项目中帮助过你的教授，并邀请其访华。
- **相关主题词汇**：`study abroad program` (留学项目)， `ancient architecture` (古代建筑)， `repay` (报答)。
- **信件目的功能句框架**
  - **目的**：`I am quite pleased to recall [过去的一段经历]. Today, I am writing for the purpose of [目的1] and [目的2].`
    > `I am quite pleased to recall the summer when I attended the study abroad program in your country. Today, I am writing for the purpose of extending my gratitude and inviting you to visit China.`
- **内容阐述功能句框架**
  - **表达感谢（强调句）**：`To begin with, it was your [具体帮助] that enabled me to [达成的积极结果]. Thus, I am deeply grateful to you for your help.`
    > `To begin with, it was your tremendous help that enabled me to enjoy a comfortable and fruitful trip of learning. Thus, I am deeply grateful to you for your help.`
  - **提出邀请**：`In addition, I know that you are fond of [对方的兴趣]. Accordingly, I deem that it is advisable for you to [建议行动] in your spare time.`
    > `In addition, I know that you are fond of China’s ancient architecture. Accordingly, I deem that it is advisable for you to visit in person the Great Wall, the Summer Palace, the Forbidden City and so on in your spare time.`
- **结尾表达功能句框架**
  - **结尾**：`I am looking forward to your favorable reply at your earliest convenience so that I can repay you.`

## 冲刺背诵5：建议信（建议+说明）

- **题目要求**：为参加中国文化演讲比赛的外国朋友建议选题。
- **相关主题词汇**：`speech contest` (演讲比赛)， `time-honored history` (悠久历史)， `distinctive experience` (独特体验)。
- **信件目的功能句框架**
  - **目的**：`So glad to hear that [对方背景]. I am writing to [信件目的].`
    > `So glad to hear that you are going to participate in a speech contest on Chinese culture. I am writing to suggest a topic.`
- **内容阐述功能句框架**
  - **提出建议**：`To begin with, it is advisable for you to [建议行动] since [原因1] and I know that [原因2].`
    > `To begin with, it is advisable for you to choose China’s ancient architecture as your topic since China is characterized by its time-honored history and I know that you are fond of China’s ancient buildings.`
  - **补充建议**：`In addition, you are supposed to [进一步行动], which will bring you [好处1], arouse your passion for [相关领域], and help you [好处2].`

- **结尾表达功能句框架**
  - **结尾**：`Finally, if you have any other questions, please [联系方式] and I will be available [你的空闲时间].`

## 冲刺背诵6：道歉信（道歉+建议）

- **题目要求**：因无法接机向即将来访的外国朋友道歉并提出解决方案。
- **相关主题词汇**：`pick you up` (接你)， `national entrance exams` (国家入学考试)， `carelessness` (粗心)， `have no option but to` (别无选择，只能)。
- **信件目的功能句框架**
  - **目的**：`I am quite pleased to hear that [对方计划] and I originally planned to [你的原计划]. However, I am writing for the purpose of making an apology since [道歉原因].`
    > `I am quite pleased to hear that you are coming to China and I originally planned to pick you up. However, I am writing for the purpose of making an apology since I am afraid I can’t go.`
- **内容阐述功能句框架**
  - **解释原因**：`You know that during the past [时间段], I have been exceedingly busy because I allocated nearly all my time and energy to [正在忙的事], which is of great significance to me.`
    > `You know that during the past half year, I have been exceedingly busy because I allocated nearly all my time and energy to preparing for the national entrance exams for postgraduate studies, which is of great significance to me.`
  - **承认过失**：`Then, due to my carelessness, I did not notice that [冲突事件] until [发现时间].`
    > `Then, due to my carelessness, I did not notice that the date of the test is the day when you will arrive in China until two minutes ago.`
  - **提出解决方案**：`Accordingly, I have no option but to ask [替代人选] to [替代行动].`
    > `Accordingly, I have no option but to ask my best friend, Sky, to pick you up at the airport this Saturday.`
- **结尾补偿功能句框架**
  - **结尾**：`I am deeply sorry and I plan to [补偿计划] after [某个时间点].`

## 冲刺背诵7：申请信（申请+说明）

- **题目要求**：写信申请文化交流中心的志愿者职位并说明资格。
- **相关主题词汇**：`cultural communication` (文化交流)， `qualified` (有资格的)， `professional knowledge` (专业知识)， `international conferences` (国际会议)。
- **信件目的功能句框架**
  - **目的**：`As a [你的身份] who is quite interested in [相关领域], I am writing for the purpose of applying for [职位名称] in [机构名称].`
    > `As a senior student from Peking University who is quite interested in cultural communication, I am writing for the purpose of applying for the volunteer position in your center.`
- **内容阐述功能句框架**
  - **说明资格**：`I am convinced that I am qualified for this position for two reasons.`
  - **原因一（专业知识）**：`To begin with, during my college years, I have devoted nearly all my time and energy to my major, [专业名称]. It was my hard work that enabled me to accumulate adequate professional knowledge and skills.`
    > `To begin with, during my college years, I have devoted nearly all my time and energy to my major, Cross-cultural Exchange. It was my hard work that enabled me to accumulate adequate professional knowledge and skills.`
  - **原因二（相关经验）**：`In addition, compared with others, I possess richer experience in [相关领域], since I have served as a [角色] for [具体经历].`
    > `In addition, compared with others, I possess richer experience in communicating with foreigners, since I have served as a volunteer for several international conferences.`
- **结尾承诺功能句框架**
  - **结尾**：`If I am lucky enough to be [职位] for your organization, I will do my utmost to fulfill my duties.`

## 冲刺背诵8：推荐信（建议+说明）

- **题目要求**：为计划来华旅游并对中国传统节日感兴趣的外国朋友推荐一个节日。
- **相关主题词汇**：`traditional festivals` (传统节日)， `the most significant` (最重要的)， `diverse traditional food` (多样的传统食物)。
- **信件目的功能句框架**
  - **目的**：`So glad to hear that [对方计划]. Today, I am writing for the purpose of making a recommendation.`
    > `So glad to hear that you want to travel to China and take part in certain festival celebration activities. Today, I am writing for the purpose of making a recommendation.`
- **内容阐述功能句框架**
  - **背景介绍**：`China is characterized by its [特点1] and [特点2], such as [例子1], [例子2], and [例子3].`
    > `China is characterized by its time-honored history and a variety of traditional festivals, such as the Spring Festival, the Mid-Autumn Day, and the Dragon Boat Festival.`
  - **提出具体推荐**：`It is advisable for you to come to [地点] during [推荐节日] which is the most significant of Chinese holidays.`
    > `It is advisable for you to come to Beijing, the capital of China, during the Spring Festival which is the most significant of Chinese holidays.`
  - **阐述推荐理由**：`Participating in relevant activities can bring you opportunities to [好处1], arouse your passion for [相关领域], and help you [好处2].`
    > `Participating in relevant activities can bring you opportunities to enjoy diverse traditional food, arouse your passion for Chinese culture, and help you enjoy a unique experience.`
- **结尾祝愿功能句框架**
  - **结尾**：`I am convinced that you will enjoy a comfortable and fruitful trip of [相关主题].`

## 冲刺背诵9：建议鼓励信（建议+鼓励）

- **题目要求**：回复考试失利的朋友邮件，给予鼓励和建议。
- **相关主题词汇**：`midterm exam` (期中考试)， `full preparation` (充分准备)， `accumulate adequate knowledge` (积累足够知识)， `success derives from failure` (失败是成功之母)。
- **信件目的功能句框架**
  - **目的**：`I am deeply sorry to hear that [对方困境]. Today, I am writing for the purpose of encouraging you and sharing my suggestions.`
    > `I am deeply sorry to hear that you did not do well in your recent maths midterm exam. Today, I am writing for the purpose of encouraging you and sharing my suggestions.`
- **内容阐述功能句框架**
  - **建议一（强调准备）**：`To begin with, it is advisable for you to [建议行动] since it is [该行动] that enables you to [达成的效果].`
    > `To begin with, it is advisable for you to prepare in advance since it is full preparation that enables you to accumulate adequate knowledge and skills for the test.`
  - **个人案例**：`A case in point is myself. Compared with others, I am not smarter, but [积极行动] can bring me [好处1], arouse my passion for [相关领域], and help me [好处2].`
    > `A case in point is myself. Compared with others, I am not smarter, but full preparation can bring me confidence, arouse my passion for learning, and help me succeed in maths tests.`
  - **建议二（总结经验）**：`In addition, it is of great necessity for you to [建议行动] as [道理].`
    > `In addition, it is of great necessity for you to summarize your errors as success derives from failure.`
- **结尾鼓励功能句框架**
  - **结尾**：`To conclude, I am convinced that if you do your utmost to [建议行动], you will [积极结果].`

## 冲刺背诵10：会议纪要（新大纲）

- **题目要求**：为班级召开的关于防止食物浪费的会议撰写纪要。
- **相关主题词汇**：`prevent food waste` (防止食物浪费)， `cherish food` (珍惜食物)， `leftovers` (剩饭剩菜)， `Minutes recorder` (记录人)。
- **纪要目的功能句框架**
  - **目的**：`This meeting was for the purpose of [会议主题] and finally all participants came to the same conclusion.`
    > `This meeting was for the purpose of discussing how to prevent food waste in everyday life and finally all participants came to the same conclusion.`
- **内容阐述功能句框架**
  - **基本信息**：`The meeting began at [时间] on [日期] in [地点] and lasted for [时长].`
    > `The meeting began at 14:00 on June 20 in Classroom 201 and lasted for two hours.`
  - **问题提出**：`[发言人] raised the question and pointed out that compared with the past, [现状描述] and thus [导致的现象].`
    > `Professor Sky raised the question and pointed out that compared with the past, we enjoy a richer life and thus an increasing number of youngsters do not cherish food.`
  - **集体共识**：`Then, all [与会者] agreed that this phenomenon is particularly worth attention.`
    > `Then, all classmates agreed that this phenomenon is particularly worth attention.`
  - **讨论观点**：`We deemed that it is of great necessity for us to [应采取的行动].`
    > `We deemed that it is of great necessity for us to stop wasting not only food, but also time, electricity, water, and the like.`
- **结论记录功能句框架**
  - **结论**：`The conclusion is that we are supposed to take immediate actions to [会议主题], such as [具体措施1] and [具体措施2].`
    > `The conclusion is that we are supposed to take immediate actions to prevent food waste in daily life, such as only buying what we need and not throwing away leftovers.`
  - **落款**：`Minutes recorder: [你的名字]`
    > `Minutes recorder: Li Ming`
