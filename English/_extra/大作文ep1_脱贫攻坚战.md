# "积极现象万能原因"功能句库

## 1 核心概念标准化表达（杜绝错误）

1. **脱贫攻坚战**
    - **✅ 正确表达**：`the battle against poverty` / `poverty alleviation`
    - **✅ 成功表达**：`the success in poverty alleviation` / `the victory in the battle against poverty`
    - **❌ 避免错误**：`against poverty` (缺少核心词battle/success)， `won the success of against poverty` (语法混乱)

2. **基础设施建设**
    - **✅ 正确表达**：`infrastructure` (不可数名词，无需加s) / `public infrastructure` / `public facilities`
    - **✅ 发展表达**：`the development of infrastructure` / `infrastructure has been improved`

3. **政府宣传与推广**
    - **✅ 正确表达**：`the government` (记住要加the!)
    - **✅ 推广/宣传**：`promote` / `raise public awareness of...`

## 2 三大功能句模块化套用

你可以像搭积木一样，将下列模块组合成完整的分析段落。

### 2.1 模块一：阐述经济基础（第一原因）

- **功能句框架**:
    > **China is characterized by economic boom and prosperity, which enables people to [产生的新需求].**

- **实战套用（三选一或组合使用）**:
    1. **(消费升级)** `...which enables people to have extra money to consume/pursue a healthier lifestyle.`
    2. **(观念改变)** `...which enables people to shift their focus from merely making a living to improving their quality of life.`
    3. **(能力提升)** `...which enables people to invest more in their personal well-being and hobbies.`

### 2.2 模块二：阐述政策与基建（第二原因）

- **功能句框架**:
    > **The success in poverty alleviation has led to significant improvements in infrastructure, which provides people with [具体的好处].**

- **实战套用（三选一或组合使用）**:
    1. **(提供场所)** `...which provides people with accessible venues/facilities for [主题活动，如 exercising, reading].`
    2. **(创造机会)** `...which brings people the opportunities to [参与的活动].`
    3. **(激发热情)** `...which inspires people's passion for [相关领域].`

### 2.3 模块三：提出建议（结尾段）

- **功能句框架**:
    > **Accordingly, it is advisable for the government to [具体行动一] and pay more attention to [具体行动二].**

- **实战套用（三选一或组合使用）**:
    1. **(加强宣传)** `...to actively promote the importance of [主题] and raise public awareness.`
    2. **(完善设施)** `...to further improve the relevant infrastructure and make it more accessible to the public.`
    3. **(提供支持)** `...to provide more financial and policy support for [相关领域].`

## 3 考场实战：一键套用示例

我们直接用你刚写的2021年作文来演示，如何用以上模块快速生成高分段落。

**原题：** 某市居民体育锻炼方式调查

**你的原因分析段（修改前）:**
> For one thing, China is characterized by economic boom and prosperity, which enable people to pay more attention to keep healthy...
> For another, after the success of against poverty, the infrastructure got a well developed, which can bring people the opportunities to exercise...

**使用模块修改后（高分版）:**
> **Such statistics, apparently, can be associated with the growing health consciousness among citizens.** (替换掉有语病的开头)
>
> **For one thing, China is characterized by economic boom and prosperity, which enables people to shift their focus from work to personal well-being and a healthier lifestyle.** (套用 **模块一-2**)
>
> **For another, the success in poverty alleviation has led to significant improvements in public infrastructure, which provides people with accessible venues for various sports activities and inspires their passion for fitness.** (套用 **模块二-1 & 3**)

**你的建议段（修改后）:**
> **Accordingly, it is advisable for the government to actively promote a sports culture and pay more attention to the construction and maintenance of public sports facilities.** (套用 **模块三-1 & 2**)

**备考策略：**
请将这三个模块的框架句背熟。在考场上，你只需要根据具体主题，替换掉 `[ ]` 中的关键词即可。这套方法论能确保你的作文在“原因分析”和“建议措施”部分内容充实、逻辑严谨、表达准确，稳稳地向高分冲刺。
